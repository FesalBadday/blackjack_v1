Blackjack Game

House Rules

  1. The goal of the game is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.
  2. Face cards are worth 10. Aces are worth 1 or 11, whichever makes a better hand.
  3. Each player starts with two cards, one of the dealer's cards is hidden until the end.
  4. Dealer stands on 18.
  5. Hit: Take another card.
  6. Stand: Take no more cards.
  7. All In: You play with all of your money.
  8. Double is only allowed in the first round.
  9. Double X2: Increase the initial bet by 100% and take exactly one more card.
  10. Push happens when you and the dealer have the same hand value. When this happens your bet will be returned. You don't win anything and you don't lose anything.
